My Story

I discovered my love for storytelling at a young age. However, it wasn't until middle school until I discovered the medium that would enrapture me for the rest of my life. As with anything, my first videos weren't great, and going through a school system that heavily emphasized STEM and did not offer many resources for the arts didn't help either. I am almost entirely self-taught and I honed my skills through making projects for school or other clubs. From music videos to short films, I've done it all.

My Interests

When I'm not telling stories, you can typically find me at the climbing gym with my friends. I also enjoy keeping aquariums and cooking!
